Kafka 重置topic

Veröffentlicht am 2017-12-14

删除topic

/opt/kafka/bin/kafka-topics.sh --delete --zookeeper zk01:2181 --topic monitoring

停止kafka进程

/opt/kafka/bin/stop.sh

清理kafka的数据

/opt/kafka/bin/kafka-topics.sh --describe --zookeeper zk01:2181 --topic monitoring
删除kafka日志
rm -rf /opt/kafka-logs/monitoring-*

删除zk 里面的topic 信息

habase zkcli登录zk 客户端:
ls /brokers/topics/monitoring
get /brokers/topics/monitoring/partitions
rmr /brokers/topics/monitoring

重启zk，清除topic的缓存数据

启动kafka

nohup /opt/kafka/bin/kafka-server-start.sh /opt/kafka/config/server.properties &

创建topic

/opt/kafka/bin/kafka-topics.sh --create --zookeeper zk01:2181 --replication-factor 3 --partitions 10 --topic monitoring

查看topic信息

/opt/kafka/bin/kafka-topics.sh --describe --zookeeper zk01:2181 --topic monitoring

查看是否采集到数据信息

/opt/kafka/bin/kafka-console-consumer.sh --zookeeper zk01:2181 --topic monitoring
